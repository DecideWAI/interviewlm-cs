### 7.1 Pricing Too High Risk

**Risk:** Price exceeds customer willingness to pay, causing significant churn

**Probability:** 15-25% for $20 pricing (medium risk)

**Mitigation Strategies:**
```
1. Grandfathering existing customers (12 months)
   - Reduces churn risk from current base
   - Allows market education time
   - Expected grandfathering churn: 2-5%

2. Tiered pricing with low entry point ($10 Starter)
   - Maintains accessibility for price-sensitive buyers
   - Creates upgrade path
   - Conversion target: 25-30% to paid tiers

3. Value-based sales messaging
   - Educate on ROI before discussing price
   - Pre-frame pricing context ("$20 for $32K value")
   - Address price objections with value metrics

4. Monitor & adjust (Month 2-3)
   - If churn exceeds 8%, reduce Professional tier to $14-15
   - If Premium adoption low, adjust features/messaging
   - If Starter tier shows 40%+ conversion, raise price to $12-15

5. Enterprise segment negotiation
   - Always have room to negotiate (anchor at $25, negotiate to $18-20)
   - Creates perception of "getting a deal"
   - Reduces large-deal churn risk
```
