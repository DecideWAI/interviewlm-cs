RISK & MITIGATION SUMMARY

RISK 1: High Churn from Price Increase
────────────────────────────────────────
Probability: Medium (5-10% likely)
Impact: Revenue loss of $25K-$50K/month
Mitigation:
  • 12-month grandfathering reduces immediate impact
  • Strong product improvements during grandfathering period
  • Educational content on ROI and value
  • Dedicated support for at-risk accounts
  • Survival tactic: Don't accept request to reverse pricing

RISK 2: Enterprise Deals Fall Through
───────────────────────────────────────
Probability: Medium (some negotiation expected)
Impact: Loss of $20K/month deals
Mitigation:
  • Show ROI on hiring efficiency
  • Offer volume discounts (flexible enterprise pricing)
  • Payment plans (quarterly vs monthly)
  • Executive relationship building
  • Emphasize quality over price

RISK 3: Premium Tier Doesn't Sell
──────────────────────────────────
Probability: Low (if positioned correctly)
Impact: $30K/month revenue opportunity lost
Mitigation:
  • Include team collaboration features
  • Position as "upgrade path" from Growth
  • Offer free month trial of Premium
  • Track interest signals, adjust if needed

RISK 4: Lost Deals to Competitors
──────────────────────────────────
Probability: Low (competitors likely underpriced too)
Impact: Loss of new customer acquisition
Mitigation:
  • Emphasize unique AI-powered real-world testing
  • Show better hiring outcomes than competitors
  • Case studies with ROI numbers
  • Direct sales for high-value deals

SUCCESS METRICS

Track these monthly:
  • % New customers on each tier
  • Tier mix (should shift toward Premium/Enterprise)
  • ARPU (target: $10+/assessment)
  • Churn rate (target: 2-3% from pricing, 5-7% total)
  • Gross margin % (target: 70%+)
  • New customer acquisition cost
  • Time to profitability per customer
