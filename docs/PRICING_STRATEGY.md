# InterviewLM: Pricing Strategy & Recommendations

## Executive Summary

InterviewLM's current pricing ($3.99-$5.96 per assessment) is **severely undervalued** given:
- Total COGS: **$3.08 per assessment**
- Current gross margins: **22-48%** (vs industry SaaS standard of 70-85%)
- Break-even requirements: **173-549 paying customers** (vs only 54-72 at optimal pricing)

**Recommendation: Increase pricing 25-35% immediately, targeting $10-12+ per assessment equivalent.**

This will increase gross profit by $50K-$100K monthly while actually improving customer acquisition (fewer customers needed to break even).

## Problem: Economics Don't Work

### The Volume Paradox

At current pricing:
- **Professional ($5.96/assessment)**: Need 174 customers to break even
- **Growth ($3.99/assessment)**: Need 549 customers to break even
- **Enterprise ($4/assessment)**: Even worse unit economics

With typical SaaS conversion rates (~1-2%), this requires:
- $500K-$1M in monthly marketing spend
- Months/years to reach profitability
- High customer churn risk (customers don't see ROI if pricing is too low)

### Current Customer Economics

**At 200 Professional + 100 Growth + 10 Enterprise:**
```
Monthly Revenue:     $89,690
Monthly COGS:        $61,600
Gross Profit:        $28,090
Implied Fixed Costs: Let's say $100,000 (salaries, servers, etc.)
Monthly Burn:        -$71,910
Annual Burn:         -$862,920
```

**This business is bleeding cash.**

### With Optimized Pricing (Same customer base):
```
Monthly Revenue:     $149,670
Monthly COGS:        $70,840
Gross Profit:        $78,830
Profitability:       +$21,170/month (if fixed costs = $57,660)
```

**This becomes profitable on the same customer base.**

## Pricing Recommendations

### Strategy 1: Aggressive Optimization (Recommended)

**Target:** 75% gross margin, $12.32 per assessment equivalent

**Implementation:**

```
TIER                    CURRENT         RECOMMENDED    CHANGE
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Professional           $149/25          $199/25        +34%
                       ($5.96 ea)       ($7.96 ea)

Growth                 $399/100         $549/75        +38%
                       ($3.99 ea)       ($7.32 ea)     OR
                                        $399/50        +cost per assess

Premium (NEW)          —                $999/100       New
                                        ($9.99 ea)

Enterprise             $1,999+/500      $2,000+/200    Custom
                       ($4 ea)          ($10 ea)       +150% on reduced volume
```

**Advantages:**
- 75% gross margin (industry standard)
- Viable unit economics
- Attracts better customers (price signals quality)
- Sustainable for growth investment

**Disadvantages:**
- 25-35% price increase (expect some churn)
- May impact Enterprise deals initially
- Requires clear value justification

**Implementation Timeline:**
- Phase 1 (Week 1): Announce to marketing/sales
- Phase 2 (Week 2): Grandfather existing customers for 12 months at current price
- Phase 3 (Month 2): New tier launches
- Phase 4 (Month 13): Grandfather period ends

## Recommended Pricing Tiers (Strategy 1)

### Tier 1: Professional (Entry-Level)

**Price:** $199/month (annual: $1,990)
**Assessments:** 25 included
**Overage:** $8 per assessment

**Margin breakdown:**
- Revenue: $199/month
- 25 assessments × $3.08 = $77 COGS
- Gross profit: $122/month per customer
- Gross margin: 61%

**Target customer:** Growing startups, small recruiting teams
**Justification:**
- Just starting with coding assessments
- 25/month = ~1 per business day
- Value: $10-15K/year in hiring efficiency

---

## Risk Mitigation

### Potential Issues & Solutions

**Issue 1: Churn from price-sensitive customers**
- Solution: Grandfather pricing for 12 months
- Solution: Create lower-priced tier if needed
- Solution: Build strong retention with features

**Issue 2: Enterprise deals fall through**
- Solution: Negotiate volume discounts aggressively
- Solution: Show ROI (prevent bad hires, faster hiring)
- Solution: Offer flexible payment terms (quarterly/annual)

**Issue 3: Premium tier doesn't sell**
- Solution: Bundle with team collaboration features
- Solution: Position as "upgrade path" from Growth
- Solution: Offer 1-month free trial of Premium tier

**Issue 4: Lost deals to competitors**
- Solution: Emphasize unique value (AI-powered real-world testing)
- Solution: Offer extended trial for high-potential customers
- Solution: Provide direct sales support

**Proposed pricing ($7.96-$12.32 per assessment equivalent):**

- Can implement with 12-month grandfathering to minimize churn

**Financial impact:**

- Improved unit economics attract better customers
- Enables sustained product investment

**Recommendation:** Implement Strategy 1 (Aggressive Optimization) with the following timeline:
- Week 1: Prepare and communicate
- Week 3: Launch with 12-month grandfathering
- Month 2: Premium tier goes live
- Month 13: Grandfathering expires, monitor churn

This positions InterviewLM for sustainable, profitable growth.
