## ONE-PAGE DECISION FRAMEWORK

### The Opportunity

InterviewLM delivers **$25K-$50K of measurable value per assessment** but prices at only **$10**—representing 0.02-0.04% of value delivered.

**Competitive situation:**
- Competitors price $12-25/assessment (lower value)
- InterviewLM unique in AI proficiency measurement
- Market recognizing AI proficiency as critical skill
- First-mover window: 18-36 months before serious competition

### The Recommendation

**Transition from cost-plus pricing ($10) to value-based pricing ($15-20)**

| Metric | Current | Recommended | Impact |
|--------|---------|-------------|--------|
| **Anchor Price** | $10 | $20 | Premium signal |
| **Gross Margin** | 30-60% | 60-65% | Sustainable growth |
| **Monthly Profit** | $28K | $85K+ | +$60K/month |
| **Annual Profit** | $336K | $840K+ | **+$500K** |

### Implementation: "Premium Positioning Strategy"

**Tiered Pricing Structure:**

```
Tier 1: Starter
  Price: $10/assessment
  Target: Trial users, conversion funnel
  Role: Get customers in door

Tier 2: Professional ⭐ PRIMARY TARGET
  Price: $15/assessment
  Target: 200-500 person companies
  Role: Core business (40% of customers)

Tier 3: Premium
  Price: $20/assessment
  Target: High-growth teams, full service
  Role: Upsell from Professional (15-20% of customers)

Tier 4: Enterprise
  Price: $18-22/assessment (negotiated)
  Target: 500+ person companies
  Role: Strategic accounts ($1-5K/month)
```

### Why $15-20 is Defensible

**Value Equation:**
```
Cost per assessment:           $15-20
Value delivered:               $25,000-$50,000
ROI for customer:              1,250:1 to 3,300:1
Cost as % of value:            0.03-0.08% (YES, that's right)
```

**Competitive Positioning:**
- HackerRank/Codility: $15-25 (lower value, larger scale)
- InterviewLM: $15-20 (higher value, first-mover)
- We're CHEAPER on price but BETTER on value

### Churn Risk Mitigation

**Grandfathering Strategy:**
- Existing 310 customers: Current pricing for 12 months
- New customers: New pricing immediately
- Expected grandfathering churn: 2-5% (acceptable)
- Total blended churn: 3-5% first month (manageable)

**Messaging:**
> "We're investing heavily in making hiring smarter. New features coming. Existing customers are grandfathered for 12 months at current pricing as thanks for being early believers."

### Sales Enablement

**Price Objection Response:**
> "One bad hire costs $110K. We prevent 40-50% of bad hires through early AI proficiency identification. That's $44-55K value per bad hire prevented. At $20/assessment, you're paying less than 0.04% of the value we generate. Why would you optimize for cost per assessment instead of quality of hire?"

**Competitive Response:**
> "Traditional assessments measure coding without AI—a skill that will be obsolete within 2 years. We measure AI proficiency, the actual skill your developers need. Our customers report 30-40% productivity uplift. Not just better hiring—transforming team capability."

## CRITICAL SUCCESS FACTORS

### What Must Be True

1. **Sales team understands value positioning**
   - NOT "We're raising prices"
   - YES "We're providing premium value and pricing accordingly"
   - Messaging focus: Outcomes, not cost

2. **Customer communication is positive**
   - Grandfathering must feel generous (12 months)
   - New customers get value education first
   - Emphasis on investment, not commodity

3. **Tier design enables conversion**
   - Starter ($10) is true trial/entry
   - Professional ($15) is the real target
   - Premium ($20) is upsell/service tier
   - Enterprise is negotiated (room to discount to $18)

4. **Weekly monitoring of early adoption**
   - Track tier distribution in week 1-2
   - Monitor churn daily
   - Adjust messaging if needed
   - Quick pivot if churn exceeds 7% monthly

### What Could Go Wrong

| Risk | Probability | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|-------------|--------|-----------|
| Churn > 8% | 20% | -$20K/month | Grandfathering, adjust tiers down |
| Enterprise deals stall | 25% | -$30K/month | Negotiation flexibility, ROI focus |
| New customers opt for Starter only | 15% | -$15K/month | Sales training, upgrade offers |
| Competitive response | 15% | -$50K/month | First-mover moat, feature lead |

**Mitigation:** Months 1-3 are most critical. If churn or adoption trajectory is off, adjust quickly (tier prices down $1-2, margin compression manageable).
