  Mitigation: Sales training on value positioning
  Create: Upgrade paths (Starter → Professional → Premium)
  Target: 40-50% on Professional tier, 15-20% on Premium

Risk 4: Competitor Response (Probability: 15-20%)
  Mitigation: 12-18 month technical/market lead
  Defense: First-mover moat, unique data, brand positioning
  Outcome: If competitor enters, market expands (validates demand)

Mitigation Strategy Summary:
- Weekly monitoring of key metrics (Week 1-4)
- Grandfathering reduces churn risk significantly
- Sales training critical (value vs price positioning)
- Quick pivot capability if adoption/churn trajectory off
